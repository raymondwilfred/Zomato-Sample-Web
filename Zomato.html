About Zomato

Zomato is a leading Indian food-tech company, founded in 2008, that offers online food ordering, restaurant discovery, and table reservation services, connecting millions of customers with restaurants and delivery partners across hundreds of cities. It started as a restaurant listing site (Foodiebay) and evolved into a major platform for food delivery, driven by a mission for "better food for more people," utilizing strong tech for seamless experiences in India and select international markets.
